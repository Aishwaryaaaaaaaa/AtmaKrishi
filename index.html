WHO WE ARE

We are BAM — a team of 11 students from YEB building AtmaKrishi to preserve India's indigenous agricultural knowledge before it is lost forever.

OUR BELIEF

Every farming community holds generations of wisdom that cannot be replaced by data alone. By combining verified traditional knowledge with AI, we can help farmers make smarter decisions while preserving India's agricultural heritage.

THE PROBLEM

India is Losing Its Agricultural Wisdom.

For centuries, farming knowledge was passed from one generation to the next. Today, that wisdom is disappearing faster than it can be preserved, leaving future farmers without the local knowledge that once made agriculture resilient.

Undocumented Knowledge

Thousands of indigenous farming practices exist only in memory, with no structured archive to preserve them.

Generational Loss

As experienced farmers retire, decades of practical wisdom disappear along with them.

Vanishing Biodiversity

Traditional seeds and region-specific cultivation techniques are steadily being replaced and forgotten.

One-Size-Fits-All Advice

Generic recommendations often overlook local climate, soil conditions, and regional farming traditions.

Every season that passes without preservation means another chapter of India's agricultural heritage may be lost forever.
